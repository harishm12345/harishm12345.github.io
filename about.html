Welcome to Arvind Polymers

We are a multi-faceted design and development company specializing in Thermoforming and Vacuumforming of plastic trays, blister packs, and clamshells using state-of-the-art techniques and machinery.

  • State-of-the-art manufacturing facilities.
  • Experienced and dedicated team.
  • Commitment to sustainability and innovation.

We understand the needs of corporate clients and work hard to find solutions to your packaging needs. Our team is dedicated to providing exceptional service and support.

Thermoformed Plastic Trays

Made from HIPS, ABS, HDPE, APET, GPET, etc. Ideal for various industrial segments including automotive, electronics, and FMCG.

Clamshells/Blister Packs

Transparent trays and heat-sealable blisters for packaging and displaying goods. Custom clamshells enhance your brand image and increase sales.

Thermoforming Moulds

We design and manufacture high-quality thermoforming and vacuum forming moulds using modern manufacturing methods like CNC milling and EDM.
